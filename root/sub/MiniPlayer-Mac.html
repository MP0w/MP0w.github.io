Why is not in the Mac App Store?

I decided to give Developers the opportunity to make plugin, due to the limitation of the sandbox I have to avoid the MAS. If I see that nobody will use/build external plugin I can try to submit in future (is not sure that is accepted because I need some sandbox exceptions)

Plug-Ins

  • You can use the built-in Plug-Ins (Spotify, iTunes,Rdio) in the Menu Music Services (Status Bar)
  • Dezeer plugin (beta) by Me
  • Nobody made a third part Plug-In yet...

Developers

  • You can make your own Plug-In, look my example https://github.com/MP0w/MiniPlayer-Plug-In , all you have to do is a bundle with the extension .miniplayerplugin and the Principal Class that conform to the MPMiniPlayerPlugin protocol
  • Your Plug-In can be listed here or included in MiniPlayer if you wish, just contact me at manzopower@icloud.com
